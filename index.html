About

XR Engineer and Researcher

Hello, I am Christos! Since 2017, I have been a member of the narralive team of the ATHENA Research Center where I work as an XR Engineer and Researcher. I am leading the design and development process of the team’s technical and research efforts related with finding effective ways of user interaction and creating meaningful experiences in VEs. I am also a PhD candidate at the Madgik Group of the DIT, exploring physics-simulated interaction, and the effects it can have on human perception and the user experience. In 2023 I was I awarded with the Fulbright/IKY scholarship and I joined Doug Bowman's 3D Interaction Group at Virginia Tech in the US as a visiting PhD student for 6 months.

In the past, I have designed and developed the team’s authoring and experiencing systems for digital interactive narratives in cultural heritage through web and VR applications. That was an ideal way for me to satisfy my passion for creating usable and easy to learn user interfaces, for interactive and engaging user experiences with educational value based on storytelling. In the meantime, I got a Master's degree in Computer Science, where I also met Virtual Reality (VR). It was love on first sight, and after completing my first VR related assignment, I continued my relationship with it in the team's research projects, such as EMOTIVE and BRIDGES, as well as my master's thesis.
